ABOUT

Planar Gazer is an independent video game development studio located in Springfield, MO. One of the foundational principles of this studio is the belief that games are for everyone, and so one of our chief aims is to ensure that every title we produce is available on the more universal platforms (PC, Mac, Linux) for however much the customer is comfortable paying. Should we ever deviate from this philosophy, please promptly inform the relevant authorities.

GAMES

Squirgle




A dazzling ballet of color, form, sound and arithmetic, Squirgle is a fast-paced puzzle game meant for brief moments and all whereabouts. The extremely short nature of its individual games means it can be played in practically any context--either alone or against a friend (or enemy) on the other end of your device. Just try and bring a pair of headphones.

Featuring original music by NaN.

Available June 26th for iOS, Android, PC, Mac and Linux
